About Me

Hi, I'm Barnoor Kaur, a Biomedical Science student passionate about understanding the biological mechanisms that influence human health.

My academic interests lie at the intersection of molecular biology, physiology, nutrition science, and disease prevention. I am particularly interested in how research can be translated into practical strategies that improve health outcomes and quality of life.

Through Healthwnoor, the platform I founded in 2021, I research peer-reviewed studies and translate them into accessible education. I have published 100+ posts across 5+ areas of health science, built the brand and website from the ground up, and grown an audience that comes to me for information they can actually trust.
